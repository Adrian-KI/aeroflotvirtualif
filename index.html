    Greetings from Russia

    Welcome onboard the new website of Aeroflot Virtual. We are happy to see you here having a glance at us...

    Aeroflot Virtual is a Virtual Airline running under the IFVARB. We were initailly founded on the 24th of July, 2020 and we were approved to commence operations on the 3rd of November, 2020. Our aim is to represent the Russian Airspace in the mobile simulator, Infinite Flight.

    • We provide the Russian Hospitality
    • We work to create a community for betterment of our members
    • We fly the Russian way

    Aeroflot Virtual is not afiliated or endorsed by the real world Aeroflot Airlines.

    Service

    The Services We Offer

    A Friendly Discord Community

    We use Discord as our source of communication. Our team ensure to provide the best and most friendliest communication server possible. Our Discord Server is monitored 24/7 by staffs from all over the world!

    Rank System for Pilots

    We have set up a system of ranks that awards pilots based on their flying time for our VA. Upon advancing to the next rank, pilots are given benefits to suite themselves.

    Picture Competitions

    We host competitions on the best picture taken by our members. The winner is given extra flight time in their grade profile and a special badge as well.

    Server Activities

    We offer members a number of activites in our Discord Server. These include music channels, games, quizzes and many more!

    Routes of the Week

    We create a weekly set of selected routes that pilots can fly and earn benefits like flight multipliers. These ROTW's also comes along with live ATC if applicable to a particular airport.

    Events

    We host weekly astonishing events to get pilots active and feel our love for aviation. Flying using our fleet of aircrafts to around the world for an entertaining time in life!
